Free Brand Audit — How much do we reveal?

The audit page will run a real SERP check on their brand keyword. The question is: what do they see before vs after giving us their email?

A

Live Check → Partial Reveal → Email Gate

Run a real SERP check instantly. Show "We found 3 competitors bidding on your brand" with blurred competitor details. Require email to unlock the full report (domains, ad copy, screenshot).

Pros

  • Proves immediate value — "yes, this is real"
  • Creates tension to unlock details
  • ~£0.01 per audit (SerpAPI only, no screenshot)

Cons

  • Some users bounce after seeing the count
  • Bots could abuse the free check
B

Email First → Async Results

Capture email + brand keyword upfront. Run the check in the background. Email them the full audit report within ~60 seconds. Landing page shows example/demo results while they wait.

Pros

  • 100% email capture before any value
  • Opens nurture sequence immediately
  • No bot abuse (email verification)

Cons

  • Higher friction — many won't give email without proof
  • Delayed gratification
C

Full Live Results → Email for Monitoring

Show the full audit instantly — competitor count, domains, ad copy. Then pitch: "Want alerts when this changes? Enter your email to get weekly monitoring free." Email gates the ongoing value, not the one-time check.

Pros

  • Maximum wow factor — zero friction to value
  • Builds trust before asking for anything
  • Email pitch is natural next step

Cons

  • Some users get what they need and leave
  • Lower email capture rate

💡 Cost note: Each audit costs ~£0.01 (one SerpAPI call). At 100 audits/day = ~£30/month. Adding a screenshot bumps it to ~£0.02 each.
