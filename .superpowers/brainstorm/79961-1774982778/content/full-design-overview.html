1. Free Brand Audit Page /audit

Page Flow: /audit
Hero: "Are Competitors Stealing Your Brand Clicks?"
Enter your brand keyword below — we'll check Google right now.
Step 1: Input field for brand keyword + "Check Now" button
Loading state: animated SERP scan visual (2-5 sec)
Step 2 — Partial Results:
✅ "We found 3 competitors bidding on your brand"
🔓 1 competitor shown in full — domain, headline, description, position
🔒 2 more blurred — "Enter your email to see the full report"
Step 3 — Email Gate:
Email input + "Send My Full Report" button
On submit: reveal all competitors + send email copy
Step 4 — Full Results + Upsell:
All competitors revealed on page. Below: "Want weekly monitoring? Start your 7-day free trial"
Email also triggers: welcome email + weekly free check for 8 weeks (max)
Technical:
  • New API route: POST /api/audit — runs SerpAPI check (no screenshot, no DataForSEO)
  • Cost: ~£0.01 per audit (SerpAPI only)
  • Rate limit: 1 audit per IP per hour (prevent abuse)
  • No DB storage for audit — stateless, results in response only
  • Email captured → stored in new audit_leads table (email, keyword, competitor_count, created_at)
  • Weekly cron: runs one SerpAPI check per lead, emails results. Stops after 8 weeks.
