Dashboard Layout Direction

What overall structure should the dashboard follow?

A: Keep Sidebar + Polish

Keep the current sidebar navigation but upgrade the visual design. Better cards, better spacing, modern SaaS aesthetic. Similar to Linear, Vercel Dashboard, or Posthog.

B: Top Nav + Full Width

Replace the sidebar with a horizontal top navbar. Brand switcher in the header, tabs below. More horizontal space for data tables and charts. Similar to Stripe Dashboard or Clerk Dashboard.

C: Hybrid — Top Bar + Side Nav

Global top bar (logo, brand switcher, user) with a side nav for page sections. Best of both worlds — familiar SaaS pattern used by Datadog, Cloudflare, and AWS Console. Most space for content.
